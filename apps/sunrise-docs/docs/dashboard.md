## Key Metrics

The dashboard includes several key metrics that offer insights into the health and status of your assets. Understanding these metrics is crucial for effective asset management.

### Example Scenario

- **Asset Usage:** 15 out of 25 assets are currently being used.
- **Asset Availability:** 10 assets are available, and one asset is under maintenance.
- **Asset Value:** The total combined cost value of these assets at the time of purchase was IDR 172.33M, but the depreciated value is now IDR 36.91M.
